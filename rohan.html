Health and Waste Management: Business Cases and SDGs

1. Health Management

Business Case: Develop a digital health platform for underserved areas to provide teleconsultations, health education, and preventive care. This can reduce healthcare disparities and improve access to essential services.

Relevant SDGs:

  • SDG 3: Good Health and Well-being
  • SDG 10: Reduced Inequalities
  • SDG 9: Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure

2. Waste Management

Business Case: Establish a waste recycling and composting facility that processes organic and inorganic waste into reusable products like biogas, fertilizers, and recyclable materials. This promotes circular economy practices and minimizes landfill dependency.

Relevant SDGs:

  • SDG 12: Responsible Consumption and Production
  • SDG 13: Climate Action
  • SDG 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities
