2. Transportation and Communication Sector

The transportation and communication sectors are key to building sustainable urban and rural communities. They align with the following SDGs:

  • SDG Target 11.2: Provide access to safe, affordable, and sustainable transport systems for all.
    This target emphasizes eco-friendly solutions such as electric buses and improved urban mobility.
  • SDG Indicator 11.2.1: Proportion of population that has convenient access to public transport.
    This indicator measures progress in creating accessible and sustainable transportation options.
  • SDG Target 9.c: Significantly increase access to information and communications technology.
    Expanding mobile networks helps improve traffic management and reduce emissions.
  • SDG Indicator 9.c.1: Proportion of population covered by a mobile network, by technology.
    This indicator reflects technological advancements that support smarter and more sustainable transport solutions.
  • SDG Target 4.7: Ensure that learners acquire knowledge and skills needed to promote sustainable development.
    Education in this sector helps workers adopt practices like using electric vehicles and smart communication systems.
  • SDG Indicator 4.7.1: Extent to which global citizenship education and education for sustainable development are mainstreamed.
    This indicator ensures the integration of sustainability into workforce training programs.
