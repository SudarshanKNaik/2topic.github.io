SDG Mapped Business Cases

1. Wildlife Management Sector

Key Business Case Goals:

  • Conservation of biodiversity.
  • Sustainable use of natural resources.
  • Protection of endangered species and habitats.

Mapped SDG Targets and Indicators:

SDG 15: Life on Land

  • Target 15.1: Ensure conservation and sustainable use of terrestrial and inland freshwater ecosystems.
    • Indicator 15.1.1: Forest area as a proportion of total land area.
    • Indicator 15.1.2: Proportion of important sites for terrestrial and freshwater biodiversity that are covered by protected areas.
  • Target 15.5: Take urgent and significant action to reduce the degradation of natural habitats and halt the loss of biodiversity.
    • Indicator 15.5.1: Red List Index (measuring extinction risk trends).

SDG 13: Climate Action

  • Target 13.1: Strengthen resilience and adaptive capacity to climate-related hazards.
    • Indicator 13.1.2: Number of countries with national and local disaster risk reduction strategies.

2. Hospitality and Service Management

Key Business Case Goals:

  • Promote sustainable tourism.
  • Foster economic growth and job creation.
  • Improve resource efficiency and reduce waste.

Mapped SDG Targets and Indicators:

SDG 8: Decent Work and Economic Growth

  • Target 8.9: By 2030, devise and implement policies to promote sustainable tourism that creates jobs and promotes local culture and products.
    • Indicator 8.9.1: Tourism direct GDP as a proportion of total GDP.
    • Indicator 8.9.2: Proportion of jobs in sustainable tourism industries out of total tourism jobs.

SDG 12: Responsible Consumption and Production

  • Target 12.3: Halve per capita global food waste at the retail and consumer levels by 2030.
    • Indicator 12.3.1: Food loss index and food waste index.
  • Target 12.5: Substantially reduce waste generation through prevention, reduction, recycling, and reuse.
    • Indicator 12.5.1: National recycling rate, tons of material recycled.
